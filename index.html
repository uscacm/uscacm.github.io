About Us
We are USC ACM, which stands for the Association for Computing Machinery. We are USC's largest computer science organization on campus, and we aim to provide as many events and opportunities as possible to help develop your professional and technical skills.
CODE
Learn how to code from the pros.
NETWORK
Meet representatives from Google, Facebook, Microsoft, and more.
FAMILY
Join a group of awesome and nerdy other individuals.
Our Events

Industry Night

Our industry night invites company representatives from the top of the industry to come and talk to our members. Our members talk to recruiters and gain valuable internship and job opportunities through our event.

TrojanHacks

We have a semesterly 24-hour hackathon meant for first time hackers to introduce students to how to have a successful hackathon experience, full of learning and growing. More information to come on our Fall 2017 hackathon!

Social Events

We play laser tag together, have meetups with UCLA at the beach, hangout at BBQs, and much more. USC ACM wants to make your life as a computer scientist at USC full of a friends and fun in a tight-knit community.

Workshops

Learn anything from web development to an introduction to git to how to prepare for interviews. Our members learn from the best of the community so that you can explore your own passions within the field and get a sense of what computer science truly means.

Become a Member

As a member, you get exclusive access to all of our events, including our Industry Night and special meetups with companies. You also get to attend our semesterly hackathon and get paired with a mentor. There's no reason not to become a member!
